Welcome to the Foodparcel project

Let us help our communities

The Food Parcel project is about helping our people in our communities who are less fortunate. We welcome people to work with us and help people where we can. We ask the public to donate and to volunteer to help us with the deliveries of the parcels. It is quick and easy to apply to receive food parcels and also to be a volunteer. Apply today to receive food parcels. Apply today to volunteer. Donate today to help a family in need. We aim to put smiles on people's faces and to take away some pressure.
